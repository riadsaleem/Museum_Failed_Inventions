Ford Edsel (1957-1960)

The car that became synonymous with commercial failure. Despite massive marketing efforts, the Edsel's unusual design and high price tag during a recession made it one of automotive history's biggest flops.

The future of wearable technology that arrived too early. Privacy concerns, social stigma, and technical limitations turned this revolutionary idea into a cautionary tale about timing in tech.
